Providing an efficient solution to ensure OUR future.

Pure and clean water is the world's first and foremost medicine.

  • Rivers filled with plastic and organic waste.
  • Clogged waterways, unhygienic surroundings and health hazards.
  • Rapidly dying reusable freshwater reserves.

We proudly speak about the precious water bodies which rejuvenate and outlines the distinct beauty of our entire world. They are the primary source of nearly everything, be it in industrial jobs, residential uses, medical purposes, farming and irrigation, business, fishing, and whatnot. With its so obvious use or the most complex tasks, they are getting polluted drastically. The rate of this pollution is rising at an alarming rate which does call for a need to keep pollution in check so that these rivers don't get completely unusable and lose their significance.

We at Amlaan RiverCorp have designed a river or rather any water body cleaning vessel, which will help battle the physical and evident pollution in the water bodies. Not only the battle of pollution, but it will also generate employment opportunities for people as vessel skippers, cleaners, and maintenance workmen in a significant number which we need at the most in the post-Covid era.

As entrepreneurs in the field of Naval Architect and Ocean Engineering, it pushes us to create an impact on the world with our work and a business which will produce the results which our rivers, seas, oceans and people are in extreme need of.
